Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

The Cameroon National ID Card is an official identification document issued to citizens and legal residents of Cameroon. It serves as proof of identity and is required for various government and private sector services.

All Cameroonian citizens aged 18 and above are eligible to apply for a National ID Card. Foreign residents with valid permits may also apply for resident identification cards.

Application Process

  • Original birth certificate or certified copy
  • Proof of residence (utility bill, lease agreement, etc.)
  • Recent passport-sized photograph (white background)
  • For foreign residents: valid residence permit

The standard processing time is 4-6 weeks from the date of application submission. You can track your application status online using your reference number.

Technical Support

Please ensure your documents meet the following requirements:

  • File format: PDF, JPG, or PNG
  • Maximum file size: 5MB per document
  • Clear and legible scans

If you continue to experience issues, please contact our support team.

If you provided an email address during application, you can request your reference number to be sent to your email. Alternatively, visit any National Identification Office with your personal identification documents for assistance.
